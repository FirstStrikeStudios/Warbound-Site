Warbound is a game focused on complete military realism from Combined Arms to Realistic Gunplay and Communication with InGame VOIP. Vehicle Focuse is a big part of Combine Arms Gameplay our Vehicles have full Interaction From opening a door to hiting a button on the dash. Players are able to play as the U.S. Army, Marines, and Airforce with each group having its own Gear and Vehicles . We Plan on Large Scale Maps and Fully Enterable Buildings.
